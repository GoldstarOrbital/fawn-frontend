💰 How to Fund Your FAWN Wallet

Five ways to get USDC into your wallet — from completely free to instant deposits from your bank.

⚡ FASTEST

Method 1: Add Funds (Ramp.network)

Deposit from your debit card or bank account instantly. USDC arrives in minutes.

✅ Pros: Instant, easy (debit card), 150+ countries, multiple payment methods
⚠️ Cons: Fees apply (varies by region), requires KYC for larger amounts
How to use: 1. Click "Add Funds" on your FAWN dashboard
2. Select your country and payment method
3. Enter the amount in your local currency
4. Ramp converts to USDC automatically
5. USDC appears in your wallet within minutes

Best for: When you need money right now and have a bank card.

✨ INSTANT

Method 2: Receive From a Friend

Have a friend send you USDC from their wallet. Zero fees (except $0.50 sender fee).

✅ Pros: Instant transfer, $0.50 flat fee, no KYC required
⚠️ Cons: Need a friend with a crypto wallet, they pay the fee
How to use: 1. Click "Receive" on your FAWN dashboard
2. Share your wallet address or QR code
3. Friend sends USDC to your address
4. Money appears instantly in your account

Best for: Splitting bills, peer-to-peer transfers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my wallet safe?

Yes. FAWN is a self-custodial wallet. You control your private keys. We never hold your funds. Your seed phrase is encrypted and stored locally on your device.

Q: Can I recover my wallet if I lose my phone?

Yes! As long as you save your 12-word seed phrase when you create your wallet, you can restore it on any device. The seed phrase is the only thing you need—save it somewhere safe (write it down, don't screenshot).

Q: What happens if I lose my seed phrase?

If you lose your seed phrase AND your phone/device, your wallet is gone forever. There's no "forgot password" option—your seed phrase is the only way to restore it. This is why we emphasize writing it down physically.

Q: Are airdrops safe?

Most are safe, but some are scams. Only join airdrops listed on trusted platforms like AlphaDrops, Galxe, or Zealy. Never give anyone your private key. Legitimate airdrops only ask for your wallet address (which is public anyway).

Q: Can I use my regular bank account to add funds?

Yes, through Ramp.network! Click "Add Funds" and select bank transfer (if available in your country). It's slightly slower than debit card but still completes within minutes to hours.

Q: Which method should I use?

Quick answer: Use Add Funds (Ramp) for immediate needs, or Airdrops/Faucets for free money. If you're just testing, use Testnet Faucets.
