# FAWN Key Messaging Reference

**This file is the single source of truth for FAWN's user-facing claims.**
Every tagline, feature description, and disclosure in the app, landing site,
and marketing must match what's here — and everything here must describe what
the product actually does today. If the product changes, change this file in
the same PR.

## The rules that govern all copy

1. **FAWN is custodial.** FAWN creates each user's wallet, holds the private
   key in encrypted custody, and signs transfers on the user's behalf
   (comparable to a custodial exchange wallet like Coinbase's). Never say
   "self-custodial," "you hold your keys," "we never touch your funds," or
   "FAWN holds no deposits." Correct framing: *"FAWN securely holds your
   wallet's keys for you."*
2. **FAWN is not a bank and has no bank-transfer rail.** Sends go to
   @usernames or on-chain wallet addresses in USDC. Plaid is read-only
   account linking; MoonPay is a buy-crypto on-ramp. Never say "send to any
   bank," "instant bank transfers," or "we're a real bank." Correct framing:
   *"instant USDC transfers."*
3. **Never market features that aren't live.** Token swaps and automations
   are gated as "coming soon" in-app; copy must not imply they work today.
4. **Always disclose**: not a bank, not FDIC insured, crypto volatility.

## Messaging by Feature

### INSTANT TRANSFERS (live)
- ⚡ **Speed**: USDC settles on-chain in seconds — not 1-3 business days
- 💵 **Cost**: $0.01 to FAWN friends, $0.50 to external wallets
- 🎯 **Reach**: any FAWN @username or any 0x… wallet address
- 🚫 **No hidden fees**: no monthly fees, no minimums

**Key differentiator**: Venmo-simple sending with on-chain settlement speed
and a visible flat fee.

**Student angle**: "Splitting rent? Send instantly. Split pizza? Instant. No
waiting for money."

### INVESTING (live, via Alpaca)
- 📈 Real stocks and ETFs — not paper trading
- 💰 Fractional shares from $1
- 💵 Same $0.01 flat fee per order
- 🚫 No minimums, no monthly fees

### FUNDING (live)
- 💳 Buy USDC by card / Apple Pay / Google Pay via MoonPay (funds land
  on-chain in the user's FAWN wallet)
- 🏦 Link a bank account via Plaid (account linking — not a transfer rail)
- ⛓️ Deposit USDC directly from any wallet or exchange

### DISCOVERY (live)
- 📰 Live financial news with custom feeds and alerts
- 🎧 AI Daily Brief every morning
- 🎓 Campus savings, .edu perks, military savings

### REFERRALS (live)
- 🤝 Inviter **and** invitee each earn a USDC credit (currently $1.00) the
  moment a referral code is applied

### COMING SOON (do not market as live)
- 🔄 Token swaps (USDC/USDT/DAI/…)
- 🤖 Automations: recurring transfers, DCA, price alerts, savings goals,
  webhooks, batch payouts
- 🏦 Bank-linked funding (fund from linked account balance)
